HugoKit is the friendly Mac app that runs, previews and publishes your Hugo sites. No terminal, no fuss — one click each.

Hugo is wonderful. The black window it lives in is not. HugoKit turns the whole thing into buttons:

  • Every site in one window — add them one by one, or point HugoKit at a folder and it finds them itself
  • One-click preview — start and stop the server, drafts included, logs translated into plain language
  • Publish anywhere — GitHub Pages or your own host over SFTP. Or both at once, with one “Publish all”
  • Preflight auto-fix — broken links and subpath traps are caught before they ship, and fixed for you, diff first
  • Theme Gallery — browse the official themes and install one with a click. No git clone required
  • Honestly native — SwiftUI, menu bar, Keychain. No Electron, no account — nothing ever leaves your Mac
